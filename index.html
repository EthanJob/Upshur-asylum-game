Upshur Asylum

Traverse through the horrors of the abandoned Upshur Asylum and fight for your life as you attempt to escape the hospital's blood-stained corridors.

When battling enemies, you will have two options: You may choose to attack and have a 70% chance to hit and kill an enemy. Or you may choose to run. But beware... when running, you will only have a 50% chance to escape alive.

Collect tools and weapons along the way to ensure your success.

There is no backtracking.

Choose your paths wisely
